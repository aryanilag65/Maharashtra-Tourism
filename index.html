Maharashtra Tourism

Government of Maharashtra, is dedicated to promoting and developing tourism in the state by showcasing Maharashtra’s diverse attractions both within India and internationally.

Thane District

Mahuli fort

Mahuli Fort, a historic hill fort near Mumbai in Maharashtra's Thane district, is a popular trekking and climbing spot, significant for its strategic location and history under Maratha, Mughal, and Portuguese rule, notably involving Shahaji Raje and Shivaji Maharaj, offering ruins, caves, and views of Tansa Lake, best visited June-December.

Titwala Ganesh Temple

The Siddhivinayaka Mahaganapati Temple is a Hindu temple located in Titwala, a small town in the Kalyan taluka of Thane district – near Mumbai, Maharashtra, India.

Kelva Beach

Kelva Beach is a popular, scenic, and relatively clean beach in the Palghar district of Maharashtra, India, known for its vast shoreline, pleasant atmosphere, and surrounding green belt with betel and coconut farms, offering activities like camel rides, ATV fun, long walks, and a relaxing getaway from city life, especially beautiful in winter.
